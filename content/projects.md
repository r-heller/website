## CTTIR

Reproducible computational tools, analyses, and teaching resources for trauma and tissue injury research — open-source software, analysis pipelines, and educational materials at the interface of trauma surgery, tissue injury, and computational biomedicine.

- GitHub: <https://github.com/CTTIR>
- Website: <https://cttir.github.io/website/>

## boulingua

A multilingual teaching platform providing educational resources for teaching English (EFL), French (FLE), and German (DaF) as foreign languages, organised as four Quarto-based sister sites authored by S. Le Boulanger.

- GitHub: <https://github.com/boulingua>
- Website: <https://boulingua.github.io/website/>
